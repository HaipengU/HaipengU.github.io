About me

I am an Assistant Professor in Artificial Intelligence in Animal Omics Sciences in the Department of Animal Sciences at the University of Florida. My research group (AIAOS) primarily focuses on integrating high-dimensional heterogeneous -omics data to advance agriculture using statistical modeling, machine learning, and computational methods.
